High School Liason Program 2017

Apply now!

Why become a High School Liaison member for NYCAASC?

As a HSL member, you will be able to see first-hand how a grassroots conference is put together. Throughout your time in this weekly program, you will learn more about how different committees such as publicity, marketing, and workshop developmentâ€”function separately and in conjunction with one another. You will also learn more about Asian/Pacific American issues and identity, as well as develop leadership skills such as public speaking and facilitation.

In addition to gaining hands-on experience planning the city-wide student conference, you will also be joining a community of mentors and peers in a series of workshops and conversations to critically engage with race, gender, politics, and social change. We aim to build a support network and community around our collective experiences and passions.

REQUIREMENTS

HSL Program participants will meet weekly from 12:30-2:00 on Sundays for an educational component, before joining conference planning committee meetings. The program will run from 1/29-4/23, for a total of ten sessions.

UPDATED PRIORITY DEADLINE

11PM Thursday, December 29, 2016

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, with more updates to come by late January. If you have any questions, feel free to email our HSL chairs at hsl@nycaasc.com or our conference director at lisa@nycaasc.com.
