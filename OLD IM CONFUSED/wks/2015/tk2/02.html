Track 2: Noncompliant Bodies

Friend or Foe: Role of the media in the (mis)representation of A/P/A Persons


Description

How are racial tropes and stereotypes exploited by popular culture? With recent conversations around the success of Fresh Off The Boat and the changing landscape of mainstream media (TV and otherwise), is the increasing ubiquitous presence of A/P/A folk. But in what roles are we still confined to and why? Is the newfound recognition of our physical bodies analogous to our metaphysical and political presence? I'm interested in interrogating how the media complicates the relationships between A/P/A folks and the larger world since the media is one of the only ways to see how we materially fit in with the "rest of the world." And, what exactly does this all mean in the context of power and people power?
