NYCAASC: Checkpoints

This April, the New York City Asian American Student Conference celebrates its 10th Anniversary with Checkpoints: Communicating Consciousness.

Join us on 4/23 at the NYU Kimmel Center to discuss A/P/A issues and reflect upon our history, action and inactions.

eMerging Profiles

The theme for the 5th annual NYCAASC is "eMerging Profiles." Keeping in mind how new media has been a catalyst to many vast social and political movements today, we play on the wording "eMerging" to convey the dynamic processes of merging and emerging and opt to highlight tangible "profiles" rather than the often hard-to-grasp concept of "identities."
